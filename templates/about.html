    About Trading Wheels

    Trading Wheels is a platform for beginning traders to learn the basics about the stock market and experiment by managing their own portfolio. This platform was inspired by CS50 and Investopedia's Stock Simulator. Trading Wheels enforces risk management, offers fractional shares, and expands stock order types! Click on a card to learn about each topic at Investopedia.

    Risk Management

    The market and some stocks in particular can be volatile and its direction won't always be up. It is crucial to manage risk and you can practice on this free platform!
    Fractional Shares

    Can't afford a full share? Do not worry! With fractional shares, you can purchase portions of shares to diversify your portfolio even more.
    Order Types

    Practice executing various stock order types beyond simple market orders. Learn about Limit Orders and Stop Orders to get your desired stock at a desirable price!
    Options Trading

    Trading options can be intimidating at first, but it doesn't have to be like that. Options can provide more opportunities when used appropriately. On the other hand, it can be extremely dangerous without the proper knowledge.
